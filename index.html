Veteran Resources in Wake County, North Carolina

Healthcare & Mental Health Services

NC State Veterans Home - Raleigh

Description: State-run facility providing long-term care for veterans, including skilled nursing and rehabilitation services.

Location: 924 NE 7th St, Raleigh, NC 27607

Phone: (919) 733-6400

Benefit: Priority admission for veterans with service-connected disabilities.

Hope for the Warriors

Description: National nonprofit providing comprehensive support programs for combat-wounded service members, veterans, and military families.

Description: Outdoor adventure-based programs designed to help warriors manage and overcome combat stress and PTSD.

Website: woundedwarriorproject.org

Phone: (904) 296-7350

Benefit: Free adventure-based therapy programs and peer support networks.
